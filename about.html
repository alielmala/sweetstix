ABOUT US

Our Story

We’re a small team of six who make it our mission to bring joy to you and your kids. We started in 2019 (pre covid) and have loved every minute. I (John) have always loved retail. My first job was when I was in high school, working at the local Spar. This may sound nerdy, but I loved seeing the shelves empty and then restocking them… weird, I know, but each to his own

Since then, ecommerce has boomed (when I was in high school there were no mobile phones and there was no internet – do you remember that???). That’s great but when you buy from one of these huge sites you’re just another order number.

I also had two amazing kids and so the vision to create a store which brings smiles by getting the best toys and clothes at the best possible prices was born.

As a small, focused and dedicated team we strive to go the extra mile with every order we receive. We work hard to ensure that when you place your order it’s treated with love and care. We realise that sometimes things can and will go wrong. When that happens we’ll take responsibility to ensure that the issue is resolved quickly and to your satisfaction.

We’re constantly looking at ways of reducing costs and then passing those savings on to you. We know that times are tough (we all feel it).

We also want to help crafts people earn through their crafts. If you (or perhaps you know of someone) who makes wooden toys, or knitted / crochet toys, please get in touch to see if we can work together selling your products through our store.
